Escape the Bunker

"Inversion of Common Sense: Survive as a Zombie"

Project Overview

Escape the Bunker is a 1-4 player co-op survival RPG where you play as a zombie who has regained human intelligence. Escape the clutches of soldiers hunting you and survive the depths of the bunker.

Core Gameplay Features

  • Moral Choices & Multi-Ending: Your ethical choices, such as healing wounded soldiers instead of killing them, directly impact the story's progression.
  • Dynamic Interaction: A deep affinity system with NPCs that unlocks unique items and narrative paths.
  • Strategic Survival: Collect and craft items in the bunker's core to evolve your capabilities.

Technical Innovation

Developed using Unreal Engine 5 and the Lyra Starter Game framework.

Our project implements a sophisticated Dynamic Faction System and GAS-based Mutation System to provide a robust multiplayer experience.
